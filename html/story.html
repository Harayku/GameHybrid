Rock'N Stone Forever

The Story of PAGASA Island

The Beginning

The year is 1975, and a powerful typhoon has washed away the dense jungle on the island of Isla ng Pag-asa, revealing a network of caves and mineral-rich veins glittering with untold wealth.

Tides of Whisper and Wonder

Word has spread to the ports of Manila, the mining towns of Benguet, and the pearl-diving communities of Palawan.

The Call of the Island

You are an ambitious minero, leading a small crew of hardy tagapagod (workers), all dreaming of striking it rich. But you are not alone. Rival miners, corporate enforcers, and the very island itself—rumored to be protected by ancient spirits and cursed by the ghosts of a forgotten war—will test your resolve.

Loyalty or Greed

Will you cooperate with your kumpare to survive a landslide, or will you betray them under the cover of night? Fortune favors the bold,

Fortune and Faith

but in Isla ng Pag-asa, it also favors the cunning. Dig deep, trade wisely, and may the Santo Niño guide your pickaxe.
